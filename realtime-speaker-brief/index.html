Questions to Think About Beforehand

No scripted answers needed — but having a story or data point ready for these will make your responses land harder.

What does the average site's tech stack look like when they first come to you?
Grounds the audience in the problem before you describe the solution.
Do you have real numbers on time saved or revenue recovered after implementation?
Specific platform data is more powerful than any industry stat.
What's one technology belief in this industry that you think is wrong?
Contrarian round — have your answer ready. The bolder, the better.
Where is AI actually working in your platform or customers' workflows?
Honesty builds credibility. "We're early" is a great answer.
What have you NOT solved yet?
Vulnerability from a founder/CEO is compelling. Shows you're still pushing.
